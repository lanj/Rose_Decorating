    The case for Outsourcing solutions…

    While 10 years ago it made sense to outsource these core services, today the wage increases in these countries mitigate against the trend. In fact we are now seeing a reversal of the trend due to the cost benefit not matching up to the hype.

    As an example a typical engineer in the U.K. costs about £1500 per month after tax, in india that price is now $3200 (£1600), add to that the need to supervise the project and the cost of travel to the country and the difference in culture. In the early days companies were able to pay indian engineers about $100 - $200 per month depending on experience, not so anymore, wages have increased with the increased use of these offshore centers in Bangalore and Chennai in India
